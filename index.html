Key findings — full trial
  • 72% average utilization across 17 active production days — 260.5 hrs of live, timestamped runtime data collected automatically with zero IT integration.
  • 1,522 stop events captured, totaling 11,408 minutes (~190 hrs) of documented downtime. Every event time-stamped — no reliance on operator recall.
  • Micro-stops dominate the loss profile — 1,330 stops under 5 minutes make up the majority of events. Individually invisible, collectively the largest source of lost throughput on the line.
  • Cycle time running 21% over standard — 48.4s actual vs 40.0s target. At 1,600+ cycles per shift, the 8.4s gap compounds into meaningful lost capacity every single day.
  • Tagging infrastructure ready — with root-cause codes applied, the full downtime Pareto is one step away: exactly where time is lost and why, shift by shift.
